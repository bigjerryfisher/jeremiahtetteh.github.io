About Me

I am an innovative Embedded Systems and IoT Engineer with strong research experience in intelligent systems, image processing, and automation. With over 7 years in electronics and circuit design, and 5+ years of MATLAB and AI application experience, I’ve contributed to impactful research in waste-to-energy, IoT, and solar power integration. I'm passionate about leveraging engineering to solve real-world challenges and mentoring others to grow in tech.
